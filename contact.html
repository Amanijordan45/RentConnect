Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from renters and landlords.

Is RentConnect free to use for renters? ▼

Yes! Browsing, searching, saving properties, and contacting landlords is completely free for renters. We never charge commission or viewing fees.

How are landlords verified? ▼

Every landlord goes through a verification process that includes national ID confirmation, property ownership document review, and a phone interview. Only verified landlords can list properties on RentConnect.

How do I list my property? ▼

Create a landlord account, complete your verification, then use the dashboard to add your property. Listings go live within 24 hours after review. Your first 3 listings are always free.

Can I negotiate rent with the landlord? ▼

Absolutely. RentConnect facilitates direct contact between renters and landlords. All negotiations happen directly between you — we don't interfere. Many landlords are open to negotiation, especially for longer lease terms.

What happens if a listing is inaccurate? ▼

Use the "Report" button on any listing to flag inaccuracies. Our team investigates within 48 hours. Landlords with repeated inaccurate listings are removed from the platform.

Does RentConnect cover areas outside Kampala? ▼

Yes! We currently cover 12 cities including Kampala, Entebbe, Jinja, Gulu, Mbarara, Mbale, Fort Portal, Masaka, Lira, Soroti, Kabale, and Arua. We're expanding monthly.

How do I delete my account? ▼

Go to Dashboard → Settings → scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data. You can also email us at hello@rentconnect.ug with a deletion request.
