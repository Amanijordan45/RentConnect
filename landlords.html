Landlord FAQ

Common Questions

Everything landlords ask before signing up.

How do I list my property on RentConnect? ▼

Create a landlord account, complete your quick verification, then click "Add Property" in your dashboard. Fill in the details, upload photos, and your listing goes live within 24 hours after our team reviews it.

Is it really free to list my property? ▼

Yes. Your first 3 listings are always completely free with no time limit. We don't charge commission on successful rentals either. Paid plans unlock unlimited listings and additional features like featured placement and analytics.

How are renters verified? ▼

All renters must register with a valid email and phone number. We verify phone numbers via OTP. Renters with suspicious behaviour are flagged and removed. You can also rate and review renters after a successful rental.

Can I edit or remove my listing anytime? ▼

Absolutely. From your dashboard you can edit listing details, change the price, update photos, toggle availability between "Available" and "Rented", or permanently delete a listing — all in seconds.

How quickly will I get enquiries? ▼

Most listings receive their first enquiry within 48 hours of going live. Properties with high-quality photos and competitive pricing typically receive enquiries within the first few hours. 85% of our listings are rented within 30 days.

Do you cover areas outside Kampala? ▼

Yes! We cover 12 cities including Kampala, Entebbe, Jinja, Gulu, Mbarara, Mbale, Fort Portal, Masaka, Lira, Soroti, Kabale, and Arua — with more cities launching every quarter.
