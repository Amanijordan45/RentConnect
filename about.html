Our Story

Born Out of a Real Frustration

In 2022, our founder David Ssemwezi spent three weeks trying to find a two-bedroom apartment in Kololo. He called endless agents who never picked up, visited properties that didn't match descriptions, and paid viewing fees for homes already taken.

Frustrated by the opacity and inefficiency of Uganda's rental market, David assembled a small team of engineers and property professionals. Together, they built RentConnect — a platform that puts transparency, trust, and technology at the centre of every rental transaction.

Today, RentConnect is Uganda's largest online rental marketplace, serving over 5,600 renters and 380 landlords across 12 cities.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To democratise access to quality rental housing across Uganda by connecting renters and landlords directly through a transparent, technology-driven marketplace — eliminating unnecessary intermediaries, hidden costs, and information asymmetry.

🚀

Our Vision

A Uganda where every person — regardless of income, education level, or location — can find safe, affordable housing with dignity. We envision a rental market that is fair, efficient, and built on trust between landlords and the communities they serve.
